A simple and realistic weapon system for FiveM that adds weapon jamming, inspection, and weapon clutching.

## Features

### Weapon Jamming (Durability)
- Weapons can jam when their durability gets low.  
- Lower durability increases the chance of a jam.  
- Players must clear the jam before the weapon can fire again.

### Weapon Inspection
- Players can inspect their weapon at any time.  
- Shows durability and the current jam risk.

### Weapon Clutching
- Allows players to carry weapons in a low-profile or hidden way.  
- Quickly hide or reveal the weapon.  
- Great for RP situations.
